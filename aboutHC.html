About Hwa Chong


Hwa Chong Institution, founded in 1919 by businessman - philanthropist Mr Tan Kah Kee, has grown into a highly distinguished institution that gathers the best and brightest in the region. Its reputation as one of the countryâ€™s finest institutions of higher education is based not only on the academic prowess that Hwa Chong Institution exhibits annually, but also the all-rounded excellence of its students, in holistic sectors such as leadership, co-curricular activities, service and research. Sitting on a 72 acres landscape site with remarkable learning facilities and state-of-the-art research laboratories, Hwa Chong Institution prides itself in nurturing leaders to serve the community in the highest echelons of research, industry and government. Just last year, Hwa Chong Institution held a year-long celebration in commemoration of its 100th anniversary since its inception, which saw special events reflecting the rich history that Hwa Chong Institution greatly treasures, as well as the unwavering contributions that our pioneers have made to build Hwa Chong Institution into the world-class institution it is today.
