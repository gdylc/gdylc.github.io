About HC-GDYLC 2020

Plunge into an alternate reality set in the future - where things work a little differently from how they do now.

A virus of unknown origin has kicked off the global health crisis of the century. Sounds familiar? Now imagine all that, but with you as a key player.

Together with other players from 10 different blocs around the world, channel your creative thinking and problem-solving skills as you manoeuvre through the crisis of the century. Power cards, experience points, timed trials, competition and collaboration, HC-GDYLC has them all.

Convention Logo

The logo of the Hwa Chong Global Digital Young Leaders Convention (HC-GDYLC) explores the concept of identity in our VUCA world. GDYLC takes on a bold yet ever-relevant ideology of using technology to dimensionally reimagine the way we connect with one another, a vision that is represented succinctly using the motif of the thumbprint in the logo.
